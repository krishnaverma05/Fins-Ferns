About Us

At Fins&Ferns, we specialize in delivering innovative automated solutions for aquaponics systems. Our advanced technology seamlessly monitors essential parameters such as temperature, humidity, pH, and water level, while providing live photo updates. With intelligent controls for fan, grow light, and humidifier, along with AI-driven predictions for optimal harvest times, we ensure your aquaculture operates at peak efficiency. Our mission is to enhance your aquaponics experience, making sustainable farming both easier and more productive.
